SO WHAT DO WE DO?

Disappoint our parents, mostly.

We make animated shows for the Internet - the kind inspired by flash animation and millennial humor that we used to watch online. We wanted to create the kind of energy, passion, and community that often gets lost in the mainstream Western animation industry.

So we decided to make the shows we wanted to watch, and along the way, we found that millions of other people want to watch those shows too.

We handle everything from development to distribution to ensure that everything about our art is made the way we like it.

OUR SHOWS

THE AMAZING DIGITAL CIRCUS

A woman gets trapped in a bizarre circus-themed virtual world with five other people and forced to play games under the direction of a wacky AI.

Fantasy • Comedy

MURDER DRONES

From the twisted mind of Liam Vickers, comes a heart warming story of "destroy all humans." Uzi, a sardonic teen worker robot gone rogue befriends N, an excitable optimistic disassembly drone.

Sci-Fi • Horror

THE GASLIGHT DISTRICT

The Gaslight District is a gothic crime drama about a family of undead gangsters trying to find their place after the world has ended.

Gothic • Crime

KNIGHTS OF GUINEVERE

Knights of Guinevere is a psychological thriller set in the far future that welcomes you to a planet-wide amusement park in the clouds!

Thriller • Sci-Fi

META RUNNER

Set in a cyberpunk future where competitive gaming and e-sports are the center of culture, cybernetically enhanced gamers called "Meta Runners" go head to head in high stakes gaming competitions.

Cyberpunk • Action
